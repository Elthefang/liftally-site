Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

Liftally respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Liftally allows users to record workout data including exercise names, weights, repetitions, RPE, and related performance metrics. This information is stored locally on the device and may be securely transmitted using standard HTTPS encryption when syncing or using AI-powered features.

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely to provide workout tracking, performance analytics, and AI-powered training recommendations.

AI Data Sharing and Consent

When you use AI features, Liftally sends the workout data you choose to include (exercise names, sets, reps, weight, RPE, notes, optional attachments or OCR text, and your message) to Google (Gemini) or OpenAI to generate training recommendations.

If you use the built-in AI, requests are routed through Liftally's AI service and processed by Google (Gemini). If you use Bring-Your-Own-Key (BYOK), requests go directly to your selected provider. Liftally does not send account identifiers such as name or email, and HealthKit data is never sent.

Third-Party Services

Liftally may use secure third-party services such as Apple services or cloud-based AI providers to process requests. These services use standard encryption protocols.

Data Security

All data transmissions use industry-standard encryption provided by Apple's operating system.
